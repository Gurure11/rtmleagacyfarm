About RTM Legacy Farming

RTM Legacy Farms is a family-operated farm located in N'LA, Uganda. With over 20 years of hands-on experience, we specialize in:

  • Cattle rearing, fattening and breeding
  • Milk production and dairy handling
  • Goat breeding and pastoral management
  • Banana plantation & harvest
  • Coffee farming (carefully managed)
  • Apiary & beekeeping for pollination and honey

Our deep local knowledge ensures healthy animals, productive crops, and consistently high-quality produce for our partners and customers.

Our Services

Bull Fattening

Expert bull fattening program focused on balanced feeding and health management — ideal for sale or meat production. We raise and fatten animals to optimal market weights.

Milk Production

Clean, hygienic milking practices and fresh milk supply — we process and supply milk for households and local vendors. Quality and safety are our priority.

Goat Breeding & Farming

Selective goat breeding and herd management to improve stock health, reproduction and meat quality. We provide breeding stock and rearing expertise.

Beekeeping

We maintain healthy apiaries for pollination and honey production. Sustainable beekeeping that supports crop yields and provides natural honey.

Coffee Farming

Shade-grown coffee tended with best practices to produce quality beans. We manage the full growing cycle, from planting to harvest.

Banana Farming

Well-maintained banana plantations offering consistent yields and high-quality fruit — suitable for sale and local markets.
